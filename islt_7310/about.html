About Me

Hi, I'm Hayley! I am a proud 2018 graduate of the Mizzou Journalism School. While there I earned my degree in Journalism - Strategic Communications. I am enrolled in the MBA - Marketing Analytics program at Mizzou and will graduate in Spring 2025! Outside of work and school, I am a runner who enjoys the mental calmness following a long run. I also love spending time with my corgi-lab mix, Lilo, who always brings joy, laughter and excitement. You can also find me hanging out with friends or at a local book club when I'm not running or playing with Lilo! Looking ahead, I am eager to continue advancing my career in digital marketing, with a focus on enhancing user engagement through innovative email and website strategies. My goal is to use my experiences and education to drive impactful impactful marketing campaigns that resonate with audiences and achieve measurable results.
